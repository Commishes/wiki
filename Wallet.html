Wallet

 

Wallet hat been shut down on october 1st 2021.

Wallet has now been completely disabled. 

Wallet allows you to have a on-site account for your payments.

You can also use it to pay safe for auctions via 2-steps-confirmation. 

It's still possible to retrieve funds if you have any in your wallet.

Retrieve funds

In order to retrieve funds from your commishes wallet, please follow these steps:

  • Head over to https://wallet.commishes.com/funds/retrieve
  • Select the wallet / account you have been using to keep your funds.
  • Enter the amount you wish to retrieve and the currency you need.
  • Select the payment provider you wish to use to receive the funds

Currently only Paypal is supported for adding and retrieving funds from your wallet.

Payouts from wallet are made manually and therefore can take a few days. 

Please contact support if you're sure that you've been waiting for more than 10 days.
