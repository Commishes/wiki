general informations

 

A suspension is made to give you some time to refresh your knowledge of our rules.

The duration of suspensions will increase for each following offense. If your account is suspended for more than a week, running auctions are canceled.

Suspensions for art theft  and traced artwork are permanent. When your account is banned permanently, no timeframe is added to the suspension text.

We do our best to make sure that nobody gets banned without doing proper due diligence. If your account was suspended for art theft or traced artwork and it was a mistake, please reach out to us, include your account name and add proof, that you are the original artist.

 

the most common reasons for suspensions are

 

Untagged adult content

If your account is suspended for untagged adult content, you had one or more auctions running with the wrong label.

To make sure that users of all age can enjoy our platform, you have to follow the rules for content rating.

Please note that the regarding auctions are tagged correctly in the process since we cannot have adult content be visible to everyone in the meantime.

 

Base rule violation

Please make sure to add all the required informations into your auctions descriptions to make it clear that the base you're using is avaiblabe for YCHs.

It's important to add links to your descriptions, so possible customers and admins are able to verify the informations with as less effort as possible.

 

Bid inflation or wrong starting bid

 

Bid inflation means, that your're bidding on your own auction to increase their price.

If you started an auction with the wrong starting bid by accident, please delete the auction and start it anew with the correct starting bid.

If you are cought using multiple accounts to increase the price of an auction, all the accounts will get banned. This also applies if you're bidding on someone elses auction without the intension to buy it.

 

Art theft and stolen adoptables

 

If you're cought selling artwork or adoptables that are not your, the account will be banned for art theft.

It's not allowed to use other artists artwork to advertise your commissions. 
Selling characters that are not yours is theft.

 

Ban evasion/ Multiple accounts

 

An account gets banned for Ban evasion if it's created to avoid another accounts suspension/ban.

It's always possible to contact us if your account is banned for whatever reason to discuss to give you access again.

 

Auction Manipulation

Auction Rank Manipulation

If you're bidding on your own auction, create a new account to do so, or ask friends to do it, to increase the position of your auction, thats auction rank manipulation.

We do not allow doing this on our site.

If you are caught manipulating the ranking of your auction, we reserve the right to end your auction and suspend your account. If it happens more often, we will ban the account.

 

Bid Inflation

If you're bidding on your own auction, create a new account to do so, or ask a friend to do it, to increase the price a customer would have to pay for an aution, this is bid inflation.

We do not allow doing this on our site.

If you are caught manipulating the price of your auction, we reserve the right to end your auction and suspend your account. If it happens more often, we will ban the account.

exception You are allowed to hold your auction somewhere else and add the biddings to your auction by your own. To do this according to our rules, you have to

  • state you're holding the auction somewhere els
  • add a link to the other auction (direct, not just your gallery)
  • comment, where the bid is comming from.
