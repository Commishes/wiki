Contact us

If you want to report something or have a question that is not answered here, you have multiple options to contact us.

For a fast response, join our discord server or Telegram group.

Write a e-mail to our support team: patch@commishes.com. please be aware that e-mails can take quite some time to get answered. 

E-Mails are required only, when you want to delete your account, all other issues can be solved in our support chats, and much quicker!

 

How can I help to make commishes happen

There's a lot of things you can do. We don't need any financial support to make the page happen but there are many things we could really use.

 

Support us with a donation

Maintaining the site requires servers, constant technical support and development as well as user support.
Commishes is not a commercial copany and depends 100% on the earning we make through selling promotions and donations.

As for now, our team consists exclusively of volunteers and the owner and admin of commishes. 

Donations can be made via Ko-fi. If you want to donate but not use Ko-fi, feel free to contact us.Thank you for your support!
 

Feedback and editing

Read through the pages of this wiki and send us an email with comments about what could make commishes an even better place to be on the net. We are really welcoming of suggestions on how to improve the site over and over.

If you can add more than just quick comments, feel like fixing typos and wanna keep this wiki up to date then apply to be an editor.


Translation

Since we have users from all over the world we'd love to provide the wiki in multiple languages. If you would like to help with translating, please contact us.
 

Testing

If you're an artist and want to help testing the stuff we put together, then just let us know and you'll get an account to the site.

 

Sharing

Yes! You may not be able to help us directly, but may know someone who does. Maybe you even know someone who knows someone who can help. You're helping by telling your friends that we need help.
