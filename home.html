Support and contribution

We do our best to make sure that everything on Commishes works as expected, but we're human and we make mistakes. If you've come across an issue when using Commishes we'd like to first apologize for the issue and do our best to address the problem and fix it.

When encountering any issue we recommend starting with our FAQ page. If that doesn't help, please feel free to reach out to us through our Help page.

If you have come across a bug, please find our Reporting Bugs page. It contains information on how to best report bugs with the system. Due to our high workload, we sometimes take a bit of time to review the issues, but we do review them all eventually. We appreciate all bug reports, and are grateful for your time reporting them. 

 

Submitting corrections to the wiki

We are always open to making changes to the wiki and it's contents. If you would like to suggest edits you can either reach out to us on our support pages or send us a pull request on the Commishes/wiki repository on Github. The latter is recommended, it requires a bit of knowledge of how Github works but it allows us and other members of the community to comment on your request before it's published on the wiki.

 

What else to know..

Commishes consists of different parts, a quick overview.

Auctions 

The main part of Commishes. Here you can browse, sell and buy auctions of every kind.

CRM 

The private messages that will be created automatically when you've sold/won an auction. Commishes CRM allows you to communicate with a client and keep track of your commissions.

Ping

Ping is a notification hub. It allows users that are registered on Commishes to receive notifications, set how they wish to be notified about new events on the platform and follow updates from other users.

Portfolio

The artbook of every user on Commishes. Here you can browse all the great artwork!
