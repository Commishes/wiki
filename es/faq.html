YCH:FAQ

 

¿Por qué mi cuenta fue baneada?

La razón y duración de la suspensión se muestran en la página de baneo. Si tu suspensión es menor a una semana, apreciaríamos que seas paciente y esperes a que esta termine por su cuenta.

Las posibles razones son:

  • Contenido NSFW no etiquetado

Cuando tu cuenta esté suspendida por no etiquetar contenido +18 correctamente, recomendamos que revises la guía de clasificación de contenido antes de contactarnos. Lo más importante es saber que, ofrecer arte NSFW o desnudes requiere etiquetar la subasta como explicita (Explicit), esta es la razon más común por la que las cuentas son suspendidas.

  •  Spam

Queremos que seas consciente con tus clientes y tu tiempo, pero también con los demás usuarios. Por lo tanto, ofrecer múltiples subastas del mismo tipo o con portadas idénticas al mismo tiempo ya no está permitido. Para más detalles, por favor revisa la página de reglas sobre los límites de publicación para subastas. 

  • Violaciones en el uso de bases

Para sabe más sobre las violaciones en el uso de bases, por favor lee la página de reglas para bases.

  • Robo de arte, arte traceado o suplantación de identidad

Si tu cuenta fue suspendida por robo de arte, arte traceado o suplantación de identidad y fue una equivocación, por favor contáctanos, incluye el nombre de tu cuenta y agrega evidencias de que eres el artista original. Para las pruebas recomendamos que envíes un link con tu portafolio, ya sea de Deviantart, Furaffinity, Twitter, ArtStation o similares.

  • manipulación en la subasta

Si incumpliste las reglas de crossposting o de lo contrario, intentaste manipular tu subasta, tienes que contactarte con soporte para obtener acceso a tu cuenta nuevamente

  • contactar con soporte

Algunas veces te pediremos que contactes con soporte. 
Por favor hazlo lo más ponto posible, ya que necesitamos más información a cerca de la situación para resolverla rápidamente. Puedes encontrar la información de contacto en nuestra página de contacto .
Las razones por las que tendrías que ponerte en contacto con soporte son: comisiones sin terminar, dejar a artistas o clientes en espera, subastas sin pagar u otras ambigüedades. 


Claro que puede haber más razones por las cuales suspender tu cuenta, y te daremos la mayor información posible en la nota de suspensión. Si aun así no tienes ni idea del porqué tu cuenta fue suspendida, puedes contactarnos.

Hacemos lo mejor que podemos para asegurarnos que nadie resulte baneado/suspendido sin antes examinar el caso con el debido cuidado.

 

Que es contenido adulto no etiquetado? Por qué mi cuenta due suspendida? What is untagged adult content? Why was my account suspended?

When you upload artwork to YCH you need to be very mindful of our Content Rating policy. If you uploaded anything as safe or questionable that requires an explicit rating on our website, we will suspend your account for violating these rules. When offering content in the descripton of your auction, that doesn't fit the rating of the image you're using, you still have to tag it based on the highest content level you're offering. This means that offering NSFW versions of your auction requires you to mark it as explicit.

Remember covering your adult content with black censor bars or stickers does not make it safe. You are not required to add them, just to tag your stuff properly.

 

Why got my auction reported? / The report on my auction is false!

You can check if the report is still open (#Pending) or already closed (#Resolved).

If the report is already marked as #Resolved, you don‘t have to do anything. A moderator already reviewed it, the report is no longer active.

If the report is still open you have the option to edit the auction and to fix the possible rule violation. For example: if your auction is reported for untagged adult content you can change the rating. Then, please wait for a moderator to review your report.

How do I receive/get rid of badges

Badges are not permanent and are standing for certain things you've achieved with your account.

The new member badge will disappear after your account is three month old.

The spammer badge will disappear after you've started less than 40 auctions within a time frame of thirty days.

There are three badges you can archieve by finishing auctions. To count, the auctions have to received feedback.
To reveive feedback, you have to upload the finished artwork, or a preview/placeolder, with the “Upload result” link next to your auctions. 

Proficient requires 10, Workhorse 50 and Unstoppable 100 feedbacks. The bages count for the time-frame of 365 days, which means they can disappear again.

When you donated via Ko-fi by buying a Membership, you'll reveice the Sponsor Badge. Thank you very much for your support!

 

I paid for a promotion but my auction is not in the featured section?!

A promotion does not push your auction into the featured section instantly and is no guarantee that it will end up there.

Promoting an auction gives it a boost over how it would be positioned naturally and their effect will increase over time.

if you can't find your auction in the auctions list, please check your display settings. 

When something really went wrong with your promotion, please contact support.

Can I remove a bid?

It's not possible to remove a bid you've placed on an auction by yourself. If you want to withdraw from an auction, you have to ask the seller of the auction if they are willing to remove your bid. Contact Administration only when your bid was a typo and the seller won't respond to you.

If you've received an unwanted bid, you can simply remove it any time. To do so, go to your account menu and to the bid list, next to the auction.

 

I can't see my auction listed. / Why can't I see adult content on YCH?

If you can't find your auction or submission, it's very likely that it's tagged explicit or questionable, and you do not allow it. Adult content needs to be opted into, wich is really easy to do.

We have different display options for portfolio and auctions. In the auction display settings, you can also chose to hide adult and questionable content, but still be able to access it by clicking on the thumbnails.

You need to be logged in to change your settings, guests cannot list adult auctions.

 

Can I have a second account? / Are multiple accounts okay?

Yes, it's generally allowed to have multiple accounts on Commishes. However, due to technical reasons, it is not recommended to have multiple accounts on commishes.

There's many legitimate reasons why one would own multiple accounts. You can, for example, have a separate account for your adult artwork, for different fandoms, or different creative outputs like painting and crafting.

The exception to this rule is registering (or owning) accounts to avoid a suspension, to manipulate the bidding system or to deceive other users. If one of your accounts was suspended for a rule violation (regardless of what kind) you are not allowed to use any of your other accounts or register a new one. Creating a new account because you've been suspended/banned, will result in a permanent ban. 

 

How many auctions am I allowed to submit?

There is not an absolute limit in the number of auctions you can run at the same time, as long as these auctions are relevant to the interest of the audience of Commishes.

 Aggressive posting, as in posting to the wrong categories or with minor edits repeatedly and abusively, will lead to your account being suspended for spam. Please check the rules for auction posting limits for more details.

 

Can I delete my account?

Accounts can only be deleted by administration.

If you want your account to be deleted, please send a mail to: patch@commishes.com and state your username and the e-mail address your account has been registered with.

Please have patience, it will most likely take a few days for deleting requests to be processed and a few weeks until the account will be fully deleted.

We reserve the right not to delete accounts that are banned for violating the rules or other proper reasons.

 

How do I mark an auction as finished/ how do I receive feedback/reputation?

To receive feedback and let the site know that you've finished an auction, you have to go to my account and send the picture via the “Upload result” link.

Your client will then receive a link where they can leave the feedback.

To mark the auction as finished, so it won't show up in your open Commissions counter anymore, you have to choose “finished” in the Folder dropdown on the upper right side.
